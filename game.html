🎯 Click the Ball Game
A calm, polished little challenge built into your personal site.

How to play

Choose your settings, begin a round, and tap as many balls as you can before time runs out.

  • Every hit adds score and builds your streak.
  • Misses are counted toward accuracy.
  • Hard mode rewards faster reactions.
Tip: higher difficulty makes each hit worth more.
